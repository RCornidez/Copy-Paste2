Privacy Policy of Copy/Paste Application

At Copy/Paste, we are dedicated to safeguarding your privacy.

To enhance security and prevent malicious activities, we utilize CloudFlare as a URL proxy and SSL certificate provider. CloudFlare monitors access for high-level analytics, primarily to understand geographic access patterns.

Information Retention: The only data directly retained by Copy/Paste is WebRTC signal data, crucial for device connection setup. Post-session, this data is promptly removed from our signaling server.

Data Transmission Security: Text and files transmitted via Copy/Paste are strictly accessible by two devices - the session initiator and the joining device. To delve deeper into WebRTC and its security aspects, we recommend this insightful article: A Study of WebRTC Security.

Disclaimer: Copy/Paste is not liable for the content of data transmissions. Please exercise discretion and legality in your transmissions. As this is a public utility tool, avoid sharing sensitive information. For enhanced privacy, consider hosting a private version behind a firewall or VPN. Access our source code on GitHub.
