Quick Start Guide

Get started with Copy/Paste in just a few easy steps:

  1. Open Copy/Paste on two different devices or in two separate browser tabs for testing purposes.
  2. On the first device, click "Create a Session." You'll see a Session ID appear on the screen.
  3. On the second device, click "Join a Session." Enter the Session ID from the first device and click "Join."
  4. You're now connected! Start sending text or files by selecting the appropriate buttons.

Note: Copy/Paste is a public utility tool. For your safety, please refrain from sharing sensitive information. For details about data handling, see our Privacy Policy.
