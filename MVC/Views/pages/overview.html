How-it-Works

Step One:

Create a Space and recieve a unique temporary Space ID from the signaling server.

Step Two:

Join the Space from a second device by entering the Space ID. Network details are exchanged between devices.

Step Three:

The two devices are connected directly. The WebSocket connection is closed with the signaling server and message handling is directly peer-to-peer.
